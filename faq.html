Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Ads360+?

Ads360+ is your ultimate solution for effortlessly advertising your products, services, and affiliate links. Whether you're a webmaster, website owner, or blogger, Ads360+ is designed to enhance your site's appeal and boost your revenue.

2. How do I get started with Ads360+?

Fork this repository https://github.com/erickouassi/MyAds360/fork in your account. Then follow the steps in the Getting Started page.

3. What types of ads can I run with Ads360+?

With Ads360+, you can run Text/HTML ads, Image ads (.png, .jpg, .gif), and Third-Party ads (JavaScript ads).

4. How does ad rotation work in Ads360+?

Ad rotation allows you to run advertisements for multiple companies or programs, keep your ads fresh, and expose users to more advertising content. You can set up ad rotations based on random times, times of day, or specific frequencies like daily, weekly, and monthly.

5. Is Ads360+ free to use?

Yes, Ads360+ is completely free to use and allows you to host your ads on your own server without any additional costs.

6. Can I integrate Ads360+ with my existing website?

Yes, Ads360+ is designed to be easily integrated into your existing website. You can insert the ad script code anywhere on your webpage to start displaying ads.

7. How can I customize my ads?

You can customize your ads by modifying the ad_data.json and ad_script.js files. Replace the example data with your own ad information to tailor the ads to your needs.

8. Who can benefit from using Ads360+?

Ads360+ is ideal for webmasters, website owners, bloggers, affiliate marketers, and many others looking to manage and rotate advertisements efficiently.

9. How can I get support if I need help?

If you need assistance, you can use this form here. Join our community forum to share tips and get advice from other users.

10. Where can I find more information about Ads360+?

For more details, visit the homepage or refer to the Getting Started page.
