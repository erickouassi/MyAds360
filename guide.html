Self-hosting

# How to

Fork this repository https://github.com/erickouassi/MyAds360/fork in your account.

You must host the necessary .js files script yourself, and include them in your page directly inside <script> tags.

  • To host by_random.js (Random Ad) file:
    • Copy it from by_random.js
    • Adjust the variable url with your own data (Advertising Campaigns).
    • View example of Random Ad advertising campaigns.
